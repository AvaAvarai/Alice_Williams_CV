About Me

I'm a senior double major in Computer Science and Applied Mathematics, blending rigorous research with hands-on engineering. As a Research Assistant, AI Ed-Tech Startup Founder, and AI/ML Consultant, I thrive at the intersection of theory and application.

My work focuses on pushing the boundaries of visualization-driven artificial intelligence, merging computation with human intuition to create more transparent, interactive, and trustworthy AI systems. I have pioneered methods in synthetic data generation, boosting classification models, and multidimensional clustering, alongside advancing feature engineering, knowledge discovery, and classification refinement. My approach bridges the gap between cutting-edge research and real-world impact, ensuring AI models are both interpretable and high-performing.

With experience spanning system administration, full-stack development, and academic AI/ML research, I approach every challenge with a hacker's mindset—deconstructing complex problems, building modular tools, and refusing to accept black-box models at face value.
